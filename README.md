# crunch time
### an overworked office worker's favourite virtual pet game! 

crunch = funny alien that appears during crunch-time! crunches deliver energy drinks to you periodically. spend energy drinks to get more crunches ^_^

click to navigate the screen. there are 3 pages:
- home page (with all your crunches!)
- gacha (gamble for more crunches using energy drinks.)
- shop (not done yet :p)

### gacha rates
- common: 60%
- uncommon: 23%
- rare: 10%
- SR: 5%
- SSR: 2%

collect all the crunches & have fun!

not intended, but crunch time is super mobile friendly :D keep the crunches on your phone to motivate you during productive times, or download the desktop app and keep them on your screen in a little window.
